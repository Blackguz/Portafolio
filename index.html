Web Design

Decorations don’t drive home messages. Content does. Reducing text-based content to a visual design element (the shape of the text) can result in bloated and unrealistic client expectations once real data replaces the dummy content. We allow our design decisions to be dictated by the on-page content and messaging, and often our designers use the actual content to inspire interesting elements that might not have been conceived without it

COSIAP

COSIAP is a support system developed for Cozcyt, allowing users to complete the entire application process for support virtually. Users can submit documents and fulfill other requirements requested by the support application. COSIAP also simplifies administrative tasks by organizing and collecting information and displaying requests. It offers predictions on the appropriate amount of support to grant based on user information. Furthermore, COSIAP streamlines communication with users and provides support through a customer service ticket system and email notifications.

Sistema estatal de investigadores

State Researcher System (SEI) is a platform focused on gathering information about researchers in the state. Its purpose is to enable COZCyT to facilitate connections between researchers and local businesses, thus promoting technological development and economic growth within the state. By encouraging in-state development, SEI helps to retain valuable resources and talent.
